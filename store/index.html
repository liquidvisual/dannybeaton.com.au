Frequently Asked Questions


  • When will I receive my book?

    For local orders your book should arrive in 10 to 14 business days from the 1st of August, add on up to five more days for international orders. You will receive an email when your book leaves the warehouse to let you know your hard cover is ready.

  • Why is the premium package so expensive?

    The Premium Package isn’t for everyone. It's simply an option for those who are fans and collectors who may want to drop down a little extra cash to receive some special, limited edition goodies and further support the author.

  • Can I pre-order after August 1st?

    Pre-orders close August 1st and the hard cover edition, the tee shirt, and coffee mug will be removed from the store.

  • Is shipping included?

    Shipping is not included, and will vary depending on which package you select and where you would like delivery.
