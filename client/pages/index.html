Meet the Author

Danny has written movies, short stories and even a poem or two, but the crowning jewel in his body of work is his latest book, Mal Winter and the Cloud Runners.

Danny values creativity and strives to bring this out in others. He is currently touring schools in Victoria, Australia with his creative workshop that promotes reading and creative writing.

Creative Writing Workshops for Schools

Danny's passion for reading, writing and telling stories has inspired him to create a seminar targeting Primary School children. His seminar aims to awaken and develop skills and interest in creative writing from an early age using interactive workshops, engaging children about what they read and why.
