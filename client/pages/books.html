Mal Winter and the Cloud Runners

Now Available for Pre-order

The night he watched his father die, Mal learned of the dark creatures that lurk in dreams, hungry for sleeping souls. Mal Winter, a young would-be hero, must traverse dreamscapes to learn the legacy of the Cloud Runners. He'll need to fight his greatest demons to save his family and friends from the unimaginable.

After several years of hard work, imagination and coffee - Danny has completed his first novel. It's the first in a planned series of children's books that aims to take readers to another world. It's a lively coming-of-age tale that deals with a young boy's struggle to become a hero. The book will be available on the 1st of August.
